ACCOUNT MANAGER

Responsibilities:

  • Responsible to acquire new customer
  • Responsible to manage and maintain existing customer
  • Work closely with Pre-Sales/Sales Engineer, distributors and other internal BU’s in all sales stages
  • Offer Comprehensive IT solution based on customer needs
  • Responsible to generate 4X pipeline each quarter
  • Run weekly forecast call with Sales Director
  • Make cold calls to penetrate the assigned territory in depth and reach out to new and existing customers providing wider market coverage
  • Generate new pipeline on a regular basis (Daily/Weekly)
  • Cross sell and Upsell solution based on customer requirement
  • Make Informative, true and well-organized report about customer visit

Qualification:

  • Candidate must possess at least Diploma from any Major and Should be a technology lover
  • Typically 4 to 5 years Sales experience required, preferably in IT
  • Creative, fast learner and service oriented person
  • Have a good skills with Microsoft Office
  • Consistently demonstrate proactive learning activities to keep oneself updated on recent technology and competition
  • Confident and Representative
